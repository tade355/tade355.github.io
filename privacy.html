Privacy Policy

How Fortuity Glee Consult collects, uses, and protects your information.

Last updated: July 2026

1. Introduction

Fortuity Glee Consult ("we", "us", "our") is a Nigerian technology and engineering consulting company. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information you share with us through our website, email, phone, or in the course of delivering our services.

2. Information We Collect

  • Contact details you provide, such as your name, email address, and phone number.
  • Project details you share with us when requesting a quote or consultation.
  • Payment information necessary to process invoices, handled through our payment providers.
  • Basic technical information (such as browser type and pages visited) collected automatically when you use our website.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to enquiries and provide quotes for our services.
  • To deliver, manage, and support the software, engineering, and consulting services you engage us for.
  • To send updates about your project or, where you've agreed, occasional communications about our services.
  • To improve our website and services.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website may use basic cookies or analytics tools to understand how visitors use the site. You can disable cookies through your browser settings; this will not prevent you from using the site.

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with trusted third parties who help us operate our business, such as payment processors and hosting providers, and only to the extent necessary to provide our services.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect the information we hold. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us at the details below.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for businesses, institutions, and adult clients. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
