About Us

Software delivery and project management under one roof.

Fortuity Glee Consult was founded on a simple idea: software projects succeed when the code and the project plan are managed by the same team. We design, build, and maintain custom software, and manage the engineering projects around it — schedules, budgets, vendors, and stakeholders.

From MVPs to systems running in production, we bring disciplined engineering, clear project tracking, and hands-on delivery at every stage.
